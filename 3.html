건강

In western countries, the most common motive for people to consider turning vegetarian has to do with health consciousness.
Studies on the health effects of vegetarian diets observe mixed effects on mortality.
One review found a decreased overall risk of all cause mortality, cancer (except breast) and cardiovascular disease;
however, a meta-analysis found lower risk for ischemic heart disease and cancer but no effect on overall mortality or cerebrovascular disease.
Possible limitations include varying definitions used of vegetarianism, and the observation of increased risk of lung cancer mortality in those on a vegetarian diet for less than five years.
An analysis pooling two large studies found vegetarians in the UK have similar all cause mortality as meat eaters.
